About Our Company

PKR Technical Services Company W.L.L., headquartered in the Kingdom of Bahrain, is a professional and technical services firm focused on engineering consultancy, offshore EPC coordination, project supervision, and commissioning support across the GCC. We function as a high-level management office providing end-to-end technical expertise to industrial, commercial, and governmental clients—backed by deep engineering knowledge and field execution through our regional affiliates.

The company was founded by Er. Pavurdeen Rawther, a seasoned industrial expert with over 30 years of experience in instrumentation, electrical systems, process automation, and large-scale infrastructure projects across the Middle East. He is also the founder of Federal Wave Contracting Company in Saudi Arabia—a trusted name in the region for critical government and private sector EPC works, including Saudi Water Authority and Saudi Aramco-approved projects.

"At PKRTECH, our mission is to bridge precision engineering with reliable execution. By combining deep technical insight with regional expertise, we deliver intelligent, sustainable solutions that meet the evolving needs of industry."

—Er. Pavurdeen Rawther, Founder & Director
